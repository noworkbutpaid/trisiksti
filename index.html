With diversity comes complexity, getting it right from the start, awareness of consequences and attention to detail pay huge reward.

    • Asset Holding

      Ownership of physical assets such as property, commercial plant, vehicles, yachts and aircraft is accompanied by third party liability for loss or injury. Ownership through a company provides a backstop position to limit these exposures.

    • Structures

    • Partnerships

    Trust Services

    Trusts were first recognized in England during the Middle Ages and the benefits they have brought in ownership protection and certainty of succession have ensured their continued use ever since. Trust law and practice has since developed over time to make them a cornerstone element of estate and taxation planning.

    Trusts are now used:

    • For greater certainty in succession planning.
    • For protection against claims to assets by family members and third parties.
    • As a key element in taxation strategy.
    • As an independent financial medium for defined purposes.
    • For charity funding.
    • To provide confidentiality of financial affairs.
    • Holding of Assets.

    Trusts and foundations are formed in specific formats to suit the purpose and taxation consequences

    BSpoke 360 services in relation to trusts cover all aspects from formation to general administration including:

    • Objective and structure planning.
    • Trust Establishment.
    • Provision of Trustees and related services.
    • Investment strategy, execution and custody.
    • On-going Trust Administration.
    • Accounting and reporting.

    • Planning & Protection

    • Taxation

    • Purpose & Charity

    • Confidentiality

    Succession Planning and Benefit Protection

    Trusts establish who is to benefit and in what way from the trust fund, both during the life of the settlor and following their death in a manner which provides strong defence against counter-claim.

    Taxation

    Trusts and Foundations have unique taxation status which may provide opportunity for mitigation of the various types of direct tax in many jurisdictions. We work closely with tax professionals to ensure the correct structure is set up for each client’s needs and interest.

    Specific Purpose and Charity

    Subject to requirements for validity, trusts or foundations may be a suitable way to hold assets for a defined purpose rather than for personal benefits.Trusts are a standard method of holding and administering recognised public charity funds.

    Confidentiality

    Traditionally trusts have been confidential agreements between settlors and trustees. This has to some extent been eroded by the imposition of anti-money laundering compliance and international taxation disclosure, otherwise a public shield of anonymity is preserved.

    Build / Project Management

    We provide Yacht Owners, Captains and Brokers with Professional and Impartial Consultancy and Project Management for new build and refit projects worldwide.

    Our focus is the end client and as such we tailor packages to suit individual client and project needs and wishes

    We approach construction management by putting ourselves in the owner’s shoes, what would concern us if we were the owner, this drives our attention to detail, the security and protection of the owner’s investment and achieving value and quality,

    A successful build project will deliver the concepts and desires of the owner but should also mark completion of an enjoyable experience for him in which whilst maintaining control he is relieved of involvement in the detail and issues arising during what is a complex process.

    Making the right choices from the outset is vital, from the design team, boat yard and builder to support services, lawyers and insurers.

    Throughout the build, planning and follow through are vital to avoid hold-ups, inefficiencies and build failures. Potential issues need to be recognized and addressed as early as possible. Monitoring of progress and performance therefore must be in depth and ongoing.

    We have the experience, technical, financial and administrative together with clear goals to deliver the pleasure in both the build process and the delivered yacht which the owner should enjoy.

    PRIMARY OBJECTIVES OF CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

    Ensure the owner’s concepts and specifications are interpreted in the design and execution

    Ensure well defined responsibilities are underpinned by executed documentation and understood by the parties involved

    Performance against contract is adhered to by all parties

    All variations to contract are on an agreed basis and documented

    Payments are properly made in accordance with the contract

    Financial and other aspects of the project are properly recorded and reported

    Obligations by law, regulation or otherwise such as may impede the ultimate signing off of the vessel or the progress of construction are addressed in a timely manner
